My Biography

After doing much research about colleges which have standard academic stands on IT department. I am applying to NAMI College because of the Standard reputation, Superb facilities and the exceptional reviews that I have read from other recent graduates. Unlike others Institutions, NAMI has its partnership with the Northampton University and to get an international degree is like dream come true. Currently, I am just beginning my journey and ready to dive into the undergraduate studies program. My ultimate goal is to finish the IT program at NAMI College. Once I have completed my Bachelor’s on IT, I would be looking forward to complete my Master's level on abroad.
